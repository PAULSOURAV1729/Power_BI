Sales Performance Dashboard Using Power BI

overview

In this project I have done comprehensive analysis and visualization solution to provide sales performance and profit for different products. The sum of profit, sales and quantity sold were shown in this project. Here I have used Bar chart, Donut chart, Stacked area chart and for visualization of quantity sold in specific region I have used Map visualizer. And predicted the sales for next 25 days using time series.
